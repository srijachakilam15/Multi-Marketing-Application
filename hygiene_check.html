Our Commitment to Hygiene

Hygiene Measures We Take

We understand the importance of keeping our work environment and tools clean to ensure the safety and well-being of both our technicians and our clients.

  • Our technicians wear gloves for all client interactions.

  • We disinfect tools and equipment thoroughly after every job.

  • Technicians wear shoe covers before entering client premises.

  • Hand sanitizer is readily available for technicians to use frequently.

Benefits of Our Hygiene Practices

By prioritizing hygiene, we offer:

  • Reduced risk of contamination across all services
  • Safe and healthy work environment for our technicians
  • Peace of mind for our valued clients
